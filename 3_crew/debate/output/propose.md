The unchecked proliferation of powerful Large Language Models presents an existential threat to societal integrity and individual rights, necessitating immediate and **strict** regulatory intervention. Without robust legal frameworks, LLMs will inevitably be weaponized to generate convincing disinformation, perpetuate systemic biases, and violate privacy on an unprecedented scale, eroding public trust and undermining democratic processes. Strict regulations are paramount to enforce developer accountability for harms, mandate transparency in model design and deployment, and establish clear liabilities for misuse. This is not about stifling innovation; it is about building essential guardrails to ensure this transformative technology serves humanity responsibly, rather than becoming a vector for chaos and exploitation.
